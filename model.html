Mathematical Model

Each particle i interacts with particle j if their separation is less than a species-dependent interaction radius.

Force Law

F_ij = g_ij (1 - r_ij / R_i) (x_j - x_i) / r_ij

Time Evolution

v_i(t+1) = (1 - η)v_i(t) + ΣF_ij

x_i(t+1) = x_i(t) + Δt v_i(t+1)
